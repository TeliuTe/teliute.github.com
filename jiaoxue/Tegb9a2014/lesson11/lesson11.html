第11课 学会双重循环
1、设置窗体背景，事件MsD，输入
Dim i, j As Integer
Dim btn As Button

For i = 1 To 9
For j = 1 To 9
btn = New Button(Me)

btn.x = i * 40
btn.y = j * 40
btn.Width = 30
btn.Height = 20
btn.Text = i * j
btn.Background = &ffff00&
btn.Foreground = &ff0000&


Next
Next
